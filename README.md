# FIFA '26 Simulator ⚽

A fun, interactive way to predict the World Cup 2026 — pick your group winners, choose which third-place teams qualify, and click your way through the knockout bracket all the way to the champion.

## How It Works 

1. **Groups Stage** — You'll see all 12 groups with their teams. Click on teams to rank them 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th in their group. 
2. **Third-Place Showdown** — In a 48-team World Cup, the best 8 third-place teams also make it through. Once your groups are ranked, you pick which third-place teams you think deserve a spot.
3. **Knockout Bracket** — With your 32 qualifiers locked in, you move into the bracket. Click the winner of each match, round after round (Round of 32 → Round of 16 → Quarters → Semis → Final) until you crown your World Cup champion.


## Tech Stack

- **React** 
- **CSS**
- **JavaScript**

## Run It Locally

```bash
# 1. Install dependencies
npm install

# 2. Start the app
npm run dev
```

Then open the local link shown in your terminal (usually `http://localhost:5173`) in your browser.
